Default toolbar

Usually a flexus-toolbar (in accordance with Material Design spec) is 56px tall (except for tablets where it's 64px, because default gutter and side margins raise from 16px to 24px).

Speaking of gutter and margins... The toolbar has a 16px padding on both sides. Followed by a 24x24px hamburger or navigation arrow icon/button. That's 40px so far. Indentation keyline is 56px away from content margin (16px on the phone), which positions the title at exactly 72px (16+56) from the left edge, with 32px space between nav icon and the title.
This demo is a bit inspired by the way the floating card-like toolbar works and looks in Google Photos.
Floating toolbar

Floating toolbar aligns to the same keyline, though the space between nav icon and title is only 16px.

The toolbar also has side paddings of 16px, but it's absolutely positioned another 16px from the border of the view/screen. 16px (position) + 16px (padding) + 24px (icon) makes it 56px and the title lies another 16px away at exactly 72px from the left edge.
